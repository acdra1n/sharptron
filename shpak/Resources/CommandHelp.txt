Usage: shar [options] [command]

Commands:

	add|a <archive> <file> <pathInArchive>
		add existing file to archive

	info|i <archive>
		displays archive info

	list|l <archive>
		lists contents of archive

	extract|e <archive> <dest>
		extract archive to specified destination

Options:

	-h, --help        this document
	-V, --version     display version
